WHO WE ARE

TR Morgan, LLC is a professional tax, accounting, and consulting firm that believes in customer satisfaction and excellence. Our mission is to exceed the expectations of our customers through the delivery of quality and accurate income tax, accounting, and consulting services utilizing a highly skilled staff of professional consultants.

Our firm was founded in 2007 by Tysheba Morgan, CPA, CIA, MBA and Radharani Morgan, MBA, who have over 30 years of education and experience combined in business, accounting, and customer and financial services.


Our staff consists of experienced professionals with a "hands on" approach to tax preparation, financial assistance and advice. Not only will you find our team members knowledgeable, but you will also discover that our staff truly cares about serving the needs of our clients. Each staff member is required to complete an extensive initial training program and pursue an additional 20 hours of continuous education to remain updated on applicable tax laws, financial, and accounting regulations. Our team has specialized experience and expertise in:

  • Tax Planning and Preparation for individuals and small businesses;
  • Audit and Accounting;
  • Accounting for Non-profit Organizations; and
  • Credit and Financial Management.

We are committed to providing outstanding service to each client by combining professionalism with a promise for timely service and accurate work. Our goal is to assist clients in every aspect of their financial lives. We work closely with each client to help them achieve their financial goals.
